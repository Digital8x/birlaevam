Frequently Asked Questions

Get immediate clarifications about Birla Evam configurations, location, RERA parameters, and developmental status.

Where is Birla Evam located?

Birla Evam is strategically located right on the Manjri-Hadapsar Link Road, East Pune. This ensures rapid driving times to Hadapsar Bypass, Kharadi, Magarpatta IT hubs, and Solapur Road.

What are the available floor plans and starting prices?

The township offers premium 2 BHK (707-723 sq.ft.), 2 BHK Grand XL (789-805 sq.ft.), and 3 BHK Premium (962-975 sq.ft.) residences. Starting price is ₹83 Lakhs* onwards, going up to ₹1.12 Crore* for spacious 3 BHK models.

Who is the developer of this property?

The project is developed by Birla Estates, the highly trusted real estate arm of Century Textiles and Industries LTD (an Aditya Birla Group company), renowned for quality, trust, and landmark creations.

Is the project registered with MahaRERA?

Yes, Birla Evam is fully registered with the Maharashtra Real Estate Regulatory Authority. The project registration number is PR1261012500295, and all documentations are fully compliant.

What premium amenities are provided in the township?

Residents get access to over 50+ amenities including a grand multi-level clubhouse, high-tech swimming pool, kids splash pool, robust gym, organic gardens, 24x7 advanced security networks, and electric vehicle charging bays.
