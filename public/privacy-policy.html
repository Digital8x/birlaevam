Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of critical importance to us. This privacy statement details how we collect, process, manage, and safeguard the information you supply when using our portal to check Birla Evam project insights and configurations.

1. Information Collection

We receive information when you fill our popup and callback request forms. The details logged include:

  • Your name and contact numbers.
  • Your email ID (where provided).
  • Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser details, and transit referring channels (for spam validation).

2. Purpose of Processing

The details collected are processed specifically to:

  • Enable our certified real estate executives to contact you with appropriate property presentations.
  • Share floorplan brochures, pricing calculations, and discount updates.
  • Ensure security by screening and blocking malicious web bots or foreign fake registrations.

3. Data Security

We employ appropriate security architectures (such as database access limits and real-time VPN scanning) to shield your personal details from unauthorized intercepts. We never trade, lease, or lease your private personal details to third-party marketing firms.

4. Google Ads & Third-Party Disclaimers

This portal utilizes digital trackers (such as Google tag tracking and cookies) to profile audience metrics. If you do not wish to share cookies, you can disable them in your browser configuration settings.

5. Consent & Contact

By registering on our forms, you explicitly authorize our representatives to connect with you via voice calls, WhatsApp, or email, bypassing any active DND registries.
