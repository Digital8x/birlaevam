Location Analysis

Hadapsar-Manjri: East Pune's Next Growth Hub

May 18, 2026 5 Mins Read Editorial Staff

Pune's real estate landscape has evolved rapidly over the past two decades. While the western suburbs like Hinjewadi and Wakad spearheaded the initial growth driven by the IT boom, the spotlight has firmly shifted to the eastern corridor. Leading this eastern charge is the high-growth corridor of Hadapsar-Manjri.

Once considered quiet agrarian pockets, Hadapsar and Manjri are now transforming into high-end residential, commercial, and retail centers. Proximity to employment hubs, significant infrastructural investments, and competitive property pricing make this corridor East Pune's primary growth hub.

"Hadapsar-Manjri stands as East Pune's most dynamic corridor, seamlessly bridging top-tier IT hubs with beautifully planned sustainable townships."

1. Proximity to Major IT & Industrial Hubs

The primary driver for real estate demand in Hadapsar-Manjri is its outstanding proximity to major employment zones. Professionals residing here have quick, hassle-free commutes to:

  • Magarpatta Cybercity: Located just 8-10 minutes away, this massive IT park hosts hundreds of global technology and financial conglomerates.
  • SP Infocity (Fursungi): Located just 12-15 minutes away, this IT SEZ hosts major financial services, technology operations, and business service units.
  • Kharadi IT Hub: Easily accessible via the Hadapsar bypass and the Kharadi-Mundhwa bridge, offering quick access to EON IT Park and World Trade Center.

2. Unmatched Connectivity

The Hadapsar-Manjri Link Road acts as a crucial connector between Pune-Solapur Highway (NH-65) and Mundhwa-Kharadi road. This connectivity ensures that residents can easily traverse between key zones in East Pune. Furthermore, upcoming infrastructure like the proposed Pune Ring Road will further reduce transit times and boost local logistics.

3. Saturated Micro-markets vs. The Manjri Advantage

Established suburbs like Koregaon Park, Kalyani Nagar, and even Mundhwa are facing acute saturation, high congestion, and soaring property pricing. In contrast, Manjri offers large land parcels suitable for planning massive, self-sustained gated townships like Birla Evam. This availability allows developers to create vast green zones, comprehensive modern amenities, and wide internal avenues that are simply not possible in congested central pockets.

4. Rapid Capital Appreciation

Due to ongoing infrastructure expansion—including metro connectivity lines, wider bypass expressways, and upcoming commercial spaces—property values in Hadapsar-Manjri have shown consistent upward trends. Industry analysts project that early investments in master-planned communities like Birla Evam will yield significant long-term capital gains and strong rental yields, mirroring the transformation witnessed in Magarpatta City 15 years ago.
